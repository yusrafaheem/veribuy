Demo script (30–45s)

  1. Type a query (“matte lipstick under $15”)
  2. Change max price and sort
  3. Open “Compare sellers”
  4. Save 1–2 items to Wishlist
  5. Look up a coupon code, or submit one of your own

Note

Live prices come from real-time US shopping results. Coupon verification and photo-verified reviews are backed by real accounts and real submissions. Price history is simulated for now and will connect to real historical data as that integration ships.
